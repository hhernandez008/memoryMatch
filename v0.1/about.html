About Match Unbound

About The Game

The memory matching game "Match Unbound" was inspired by the book Mr. Penumbra's 24-Hour Bookstore. The cards draw on character traits and locations throughout the book. This lead to the title being "Match Unbound", as some of the characters in the book are considered Unbound in their "book club".
